This is a tiny game inspired by the likes of [QWOP](https://www.foddy.net/legacy/Athletics.html),
[Sanicball](https://badgerson.itch.io/sanicball), and [Marble Blast](https://marbleblast.com/)!

You are a [pangolin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pangolin) who just loves to race! Rolling around the racetrack is tricky though :(

To build up speed, you must _roll_ the <kbd>h</kbd>, <kbd>j</kbd>, <kbd>k</kbd>, and <kbd>l</kbd> keys in the direction (left or right) you want to move. You can think about this like sliding across the keys of a piano, or tapping the tips your fingers together while muttering "Excellent...".

You'll start moving faster when key taps are shown in green. Tapping in the opposite direction, or making a mistake will slow you down (shown in red).

This only works in two directions though! To change directions, the spacebar can be used to rotate 90° for a whole additional dimension of movement!

Make your way around the track and see how fast you can complete it!
